# 30-Day Weight-Gain Plan — No-Cook · Drink-Based · German Discounter Budget

**Profile:** 184 cm · ~60 kg · 47 y · desk job · small appetite, prefers drinking · real blender · no fish except tuna-bean salad · dairy fine
**Daily target:** ~3,000–3,200 kcal · ~110 g protein → steady gain of **~0.4–0.6 kg/week**
**Cost:** **~€240–250 / month** incl. bottled water at €0.66/L (Aldi / Lidl / Netto, early 2026) → back under your **€270** cap with a ~€20–25 buffer.

**How it works:** two calorie-dense shakes carry ~2/3 of each day, so most calories are *drunk*. A **6-day menu rotation** (Day 1–6 below) repeats 5× across the month so nothing gets boring. You shop a small **fresh run every 3 days** and restock **pantry/freezer** less often.

## The 6-Day Menu Rotation

Each day = **Morning shake + 2 solid snacks + Evening shake** ≈ 3,000–3,200 kcal. Shake recipes (S1–S4) and Solid sets (A/B/C) are defined below.

|----|---------|--------|-----------|---------|
| **1** | Shake **S1** (PB-Banana) | Solid **A** (avocado-cheese bread) | Solid **A** (yogurt + walnuts + kiwi) | Shake **S2** (Blueberry-Walnut) |
| **2** | Shake **S3** (Cocoa-Banana) | Solid **B** (tuna-bean salad) | Solid **B** (PB bread + banana + orange) | Shake **S1** (PB-Banana) |
| **3** | Shake **S2** (Blueberry-Walnut) | Solid **C** (cheese-honey bread) | Solid **C** (yogurt + almonds + orange) | Shake **S4** (Berry-Yogurt) |

| **5** | Shake **S4** (Berry-Yogurt) | Solid **B** (tuna-bean salad) | Solid **B** (PB bread + banana + orange) | Shake **S2** (Blueberry-Walnut) |

Then **repeat**: Days 7–12 = same as 1–6, and so on to Day 30. Your tuna-bean salad lands twice every 6 days (~2×/week).

## Shake Recipes (blend → pour → sip over 20–30 min)

> Freeze bananas (peeled, in chunks) and keep berries frozen — it makes the shakes thick and cold like a milkshake.

### S1 — Peanut-Banana · ~1,000 kcal
500 ml whole milk · 80 g oats · 1 frozen banana · 30 g peanut butter · 1 tbsp honey · 1 tbsp ground flaxseed

### S2 — Blueberry-Walnut · ~960 kcal
500 ml whole milk · 60 g oats · 100 g frozen blueberries · 30 g peanut butter · 25 g walnuts · 1 tbsp honey

### S3 — Cocoa-Banana · ~1,000 kcal
500 ml whole milk · 70 g oats · 1 frozen banana · 25 g peanut butter · 1 tbsp cocoa powder · 1 tbsp honey · 15 g almonds

### S4 — Berry-Yogurt (thick, spoon or drink) · ~950 kcal
300 ml whole milk · 150 g Greek yogurt 10% · 60 g oats · 100 g frozen mixed berries · 25 g peanut butter · 1 tbsp honey · 1 tbsp flaxseed

## Tips for a small appetite

- **Sip, don't chug.** Spread each shake over 20–30 min; six small eating moments beat three big meals when appetite is low.
- **Hydrate between, not before.** Aim for ~2 L of plain water spread across the day — but not right before a shake/meal, since it fills you up. Steady hydration is also what keeps the high fibre (oats, flax, beans) from causing constipation.
- **The scale is your dial.** Weigh once a week, same morning. Aim ~0.5 kg/week. **Stalled 2 weeks?** Add 1 tbsp olive oil (+120 kcal, tasteless) or +20 g peanut butter to a shake — extra calories, no extra volume.
- **Batch-freeze bananas** when they're ripe/cheap so you always have shake-ready fruit.
- **Vitamin C & antioxidants are deliberate** (peppers, kiwi, oranges, berries) — they matter more given nicotine use, and support skin/collagen.
- **Worth a GP check** at BMI ~17.7: a quick look at thyroid/absorption rules out anything quietly working against the gain. Not urgent, just sensible.
